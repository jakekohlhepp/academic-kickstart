## Why it matters

Altonji is one of the key early empirical references for whether hours respond to predictable wage movements over time. It belongs in the wiki because later micro-macro debates often return to exactly this object.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it as a companion to MaCurdy (1981), with attention to wage measurement and panel identification.
- Use it when separating short-run intertemporal substitution from long-run policy or participation responses.
