## Why it matters

This paper is a natural bridge between the taxi-driver labor-supply literature and the newer platform-work literature. It helps compare regulated taxi markets with app-mediated flexible work.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it after the Farber and Camerer taxi papers.
- Use it with Chen et al. (2019) when asking what flexibility changes for drivers.
