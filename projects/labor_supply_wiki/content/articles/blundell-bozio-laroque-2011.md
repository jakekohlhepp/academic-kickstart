## Why it matters

This paper is useful for moving from individual hours elasticities to employment and participation differences across institutional settings.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Pair it with Prescott (2004) and Ljungqvist-Sargent (2006) when comparing countries.
- Use it when the extensive margin, rather than hours per worker, is doing the main work.
