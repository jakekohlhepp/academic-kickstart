## Why it matters

The paper belongs in the wiki because platform labor makes outside options visible in a high-frequency labor-supply setting. It also connects flexible work to gender differences in constraints and preferences.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Pair it with Chen et al. (2019) and Angrist-Caldwell-Hall.
- Use it when demographic heterogeneity and outside options are part of platform labor supply.
