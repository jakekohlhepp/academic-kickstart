## Why it matters

Hours are not only quantities; they occur at particular times. This paper belongs in the wiki because labor demand timing helps shape the feasible schedule menu workers face.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it with Hamermesh and Mas-Pallais.
- Read it when the question is schedule timing rather than total hours.
