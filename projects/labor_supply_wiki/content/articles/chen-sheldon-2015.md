## Why it matters

Surge pricing is a clean platform setting where wages can move at high frequency. That makes it useful for studying short-run labor-supply responses when work opportunities are unusually flexible.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it as background for Chen-Chevalier-Rossi-Oehlsen.
- Read it with taxi-driver papers when comparing reference dependence and dynamic wage variation.
