## Why it matters

This paper is useful for benchmarking the long and noisy Frisch/intertemporal-substitution literature. It gives the wiki a modern synthesis rather than another isolated estimate.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Pair it with CBO's Frisch review and Gottlieb-Onken on measurement.
- Use it when the question is what the literature as a whole says, not one preferred estimate.
