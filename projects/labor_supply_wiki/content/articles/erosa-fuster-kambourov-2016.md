## Why it matters

The paper directly addresses the problem of building aggregate labor supply from individual choices over the life cycle. It is a natural bridge between macro calibration and micro evidence.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it with Rogerson-Wallenius and Keane-Rogerson.
- Keep track of which margin is moving: hours, participation, or the life-cycle timing of work.
