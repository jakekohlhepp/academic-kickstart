## Why it matters

Fehr and Goette provide experimental evidence in the same broad territory as the cabdriver literature: how workers respond to temporary wage changes.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it as an experimental complement to taxi-driver observational studies.
- Read it when the key issue is temporary wage changes and behavioral response.
