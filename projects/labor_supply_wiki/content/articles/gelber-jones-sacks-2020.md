## Why it matters

The paper is useful because the Social Security earnings test creates a sharp environment for studying whether workers adjust earnings smoothly or face frictions.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it with Kleven-Waseem and Chetty (2012) for the optimization-frictions side of tax policy.
- Keep it separate from frictionless elasticity interpretations.
