## Why it matters

This entry belongs in the wiki because establishment-level scheduling is a concrete mechanism behind hours mismatch. Workers often face organized time structures, not continuous hours choices.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it with Dickens-Lundberg and Altonji-Paxson.
- Keep it in mind when interpreting low within-job hours elasticities.
