## Why it matters

The paper places labor supply inside a heterogeneous-agent insurance problem. It is a useful bridge between micro household behavior and aggregate macro models.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it with Blundell-Pistaferri-Saporta-Eksten.
- Use it when labor supply is part of risk sharing rather than only a wage response.
