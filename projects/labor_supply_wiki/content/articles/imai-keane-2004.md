## Why it matters

The paper shifts attention from hours today to the fact that work today can raise wages tomorrow. Once work builds human capital, a wage response is not just a consumption-leisure tradeoff.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it with Shaw (1989), Wallenius (2011), and Keane-Wasi (2016).
- Use it whenever a Frisch estimate may be biased because current work changes future productivity.
