## Why it matters

The Frisch elasticity remains central but hard to estimate cleanly. This paper belongs in the wiki because it treats inference itself as the problem, not only model selection or data choice.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Put it after the older MaCurdy, Altonji, and Browning-Deaton-Irish foundations.
- Use it when judging how much confidence to put on a Frisch estimate rather than only its point estimate.
