## Why it matters

This entry belongs in the wiki because labor supply often responds to a job bundle, not a wage alone. Amenities, schedules, and other attributes can substitute for pay or change the firm-facing supply curve.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it with Mas-Pallais and Dastan-Farhidi.
- Pair it with monopsony entries when job differentiation creates employer wage-setting power.
