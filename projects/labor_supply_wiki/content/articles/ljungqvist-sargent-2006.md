## Why it matters

This paper is a key cross-country macro reference because it pushes on the Prescott-style tax explanation for work differences.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it with Prescott (2004) and Blundell-Bozio-Laroque.
- Use it when employment, not just hours per worker, is the cross-country object.
