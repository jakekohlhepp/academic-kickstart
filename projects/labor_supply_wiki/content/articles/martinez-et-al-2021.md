## Why it matters

This entry is useful as a low-response benchmark in the tax-policy cluster. It helps prevent the wiki from treating taxable-income movement as automatically large real-hours movement.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it as a contrast with larger ETI and bunching estimates.
- Keep the distinction between taxable income, earnings timing, and total hours in view.
