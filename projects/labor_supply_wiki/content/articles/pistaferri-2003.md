## Why it matters

The paper helps refine the older life-cycle literature by asking what type of wage change workers are responding to. Anticipated and unanticipated wage movements have different implications for labor supply and insurance.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it after MaCurdy and Altonji.
- Use it when the key distinction is wage risk versus predictable wage growth.
