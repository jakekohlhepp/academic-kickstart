## Why it matters

Rogerson is the foundation for a macro view in which labor supply adjusts through employment probabilities rather than smooth hours. This is essential background for the micro-macro elasticity debate.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it before Chetty-Guren-Manoli-Weber (2013) and Yum (2024).
- Use it whenever an extensive-margin model generates a larger aggregate elasticity than individual hours responses suggest.
