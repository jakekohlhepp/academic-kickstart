## Why it matters

Shaw is one of the older anchors for the idea that labor supply and human-capital accumulation are jointly chosen. That makes it an important predecessor to Imai-Keane and later structural tax work.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Read it before the more modern human-capital elasticity papers.
- Use it when the core question is whether current work decisions change the future wage process.
