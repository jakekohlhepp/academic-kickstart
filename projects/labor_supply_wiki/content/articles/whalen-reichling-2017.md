## Why it matters

The CBO review is useful because policy modeling often needs a defensible benchmark elasticity rather than a new identification strategy. It collects estimates with an applied public-finance audience in mind.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Use it as a calibration reference alongside meta-analyses and surveys.
- Pair it with Keane-Neal (2025) when moving from point estimates to uncertainty.
