## Why it matters

This paper is useful because it places tax effects inside a life-cycle framework. It is part of the older bridge between tax policy and dynamic labor-supply estimation.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Compare it with static tax models and later ETI papers.
- Use it when asking whether tax responses are short-run hours changes or life-cycle reallocations.
