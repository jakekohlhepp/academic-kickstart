## Why it matters

Tax reforms affect more than labor earnings. This paper is useful because it puts consumption and labor supply together, which helps connect labor-supply elasticities to welfare and household behavior.

## How to use it in this wiki

- Pair it with taxable-income and lottery-income-effect papers.
- Use it when the policy question is about both work effort and consumption smoothing.
