## Why this page belongs in a labor-supply wiki

Some of your model context is about assignment rather than a standard hours choice. When workers, firms, tasks, and job attributes are heterogeneous, labor supply is partly about who matches with whom.

## Reading path

[The Assignment Game I: The Core](../articles/shapley-shubik-1972-assignment-game-core.html) is the foundational game-theory reference. [Matching and Assignment Models](../articles/galichon-salanie-matching-assignment.html) routes the modern matching side. [Compensation of Cooperating Factors](../articles/deardorff-stafford-1976-compensation-cooperating-factors.html) helps connect assignment to production organization.

## Link to the rest of the wiki

Assignment and matching are the theory layer underneath compensating differentials, occupational preferences, monopsony, and organizational aggregation.
