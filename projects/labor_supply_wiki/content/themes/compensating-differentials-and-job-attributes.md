## Why this page matters

If jobs are bundles, a wage elasticity alone can be misleading. Workers may trade pay for flexibility, predictability, remote work, task content, or other amenities.

## Reading path

Use [Compensating Differentials in Bundles of Job Attributes](../articles/lavetti-schmutte-2023-compensating-differentials-job-attributes.html) as the direct entry. Then connect it to the Mas-Pallais alternative-work-arrangements papers, [Occupational Preferences Heterogeneity in Labor Supply](../articles/dastan-farhidi-2026-occupational-preferences-heterogeneity.html), and [Reservation Wages and Workers' Valuation of Job Flexibility](../articles/chen-ding-list-mogstad-2025-job-flexibility.html).

## Link to firm wage setting

Amenities can flatten or steepen the supply curve facing a firm. That is why this page sits next to monopsony and organization themes.
