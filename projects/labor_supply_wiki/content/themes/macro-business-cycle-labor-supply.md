## Why this page exists

Macro labor-supply papers often need aggregate elasticities that are not the same as micro hours elasticities. This page gathers the business-cycle and cross-country references.

## Reading path

Use [Resuscitating Real Business Cycles](../articles/king-rebelo-1999-resuscitating-real-business-cycles.html) for the RBC background. Use [Why Do Americans Work So Much More Than Europeans?](../articles/prescott-2004-americans-work-more-than-europeans.html) and [Do Taxes Explain European Employment?](../articles/ljungqvist-sargent-2006-taxes-european-employment.html) for cross-country tax arguments. Use [Reconciling Cyclical Movements in the Marginal Value of Time and the Marginal Product of Labor](../articles/hall-2009-marginal-value-time-marginal-product-labor.html) and [Hours Cyclicality and Labor Supply over the Business Cycle](../articles/beaudry-green-sand-2012-hours-cyclicality.html) for cyclical hours.
