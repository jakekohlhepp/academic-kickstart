## Why this page matters

No single labor-supply estimate should carry the whole field. Meta-analyses and review papers help show how estimates vary by population, design, margin, horizon, and publication selection.

This is the calibration shelf of the wiki. It is useful when a model, policy memo, or literature review needs a defensible range rather than a favorite paper. The range matters because labor-supply elasticities are not just noisy; they are often different objects.

## Reading path

Use [Intertemporal Substitution in Labor Supply: A Meta-Analysis](../articles/elminejad-havranek-horvath-irsova-2023-intertemporal-substitution-meta-analysis.html) for Frisch/intertemporal substitution. Use [Review of Estimates of the Frisch Elasticity of Labor Supply](../articles/whalen-reichling-2017-cbo-review-frisch-elasticity.html) for applied calibration. Use [Does Indivisible Labor Explain the Difference between Micro and Macro Elasticities?](../articles/chetty-guren-manoli-weber-2013-indivisible-labor-meta-analysis.html) for extensive-margin meta-analysis.

Use [The Wage Elasticity of Labour Supply](../articles/evers-de-mooij-van-vuuren-2008-wage-elasticity-synthesis.html) and [Own-Wage Labor Supply Elasticities](../articles/bargain-peichl-2016-own-wage-labor-supply-elasticities.html) for wage-elasticity summaries, and [The Elasticity of Taxable Income](../articles/neisser-2021-eti-meta-regression.html) for ETI calibration. [On the Measurement of the Elasticity of Labour](../articles/gottlieb-onken-2021-measurement-elasticity-labour.html) is the useful reminder that a benchmark is only helpful if the measured object is clear.

## How to read the range

Do not average first and interpret later. Sort estimates by margin, population, horizon, and institutional setting before treating them as comparable. A female participation elasticity, a male intensive-margin hours elasticity, a taxable-income elasticity, and a macro Frisch elasticity may all be called elasticities, but they should not enter the same calculation without translation.

## What meta-analysis can and cannot do

Meta-analysis can reveal patterns, publication selection, and design sensitivity. It cannot by itself decide which estimate is valid for a new policy counterfactual. For that, the wiki points back to [aggregation of heterogeneous elasticities](../themes/aggregation-heterogeneous-elasticities.html), [estimation strategies and relative credibility](../themes/estimation-strategies-and-relative-credibility.html), and [public finance and optimal taxation](../themes/public-finance-and-optimal-taxation.html).
