Why it matters

This paper belongs next to the shorter Chetty-Guren-Manoli-Weber AER piece but asks a more specific question: can indivisible labor and the extensive margin close the micro-macro gap?

How to use it in this wiki

  • Use it as the empirical companion to Rogerson-style indivisible-labor models.
  • Read it when the elasticity object is participation or employment, not hours conditional on working.
