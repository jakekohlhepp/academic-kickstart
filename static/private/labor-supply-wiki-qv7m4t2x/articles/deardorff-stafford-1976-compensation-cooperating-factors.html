Econometrica - 1976

Compensation of Cooperating Factors

A foundational coordination paper relevant for thinking about why work hours and tasks are jointly organized.

Authors
Alan V. Deardorff, Frank P. Stafford
Journal
Econometrica
Year
1976

Related themes

Why it matters

The paper helps motivate a non-individualistic view of labor supply. If production uses cooperating factors, the timing and organization of work are part of the economic problem.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Use it as older background for organizational labor-supply themes.
  • Pair it with assignment and matching references when workers and tasks are complements.
