Why it matters

This paper deepens the taxi-driver debate by focusing directly on reference-dependent preferences. It is useful for separating preference interpretations from measurement and selection issues.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Read it with Farber (2005) and Farber (2015).
  • Use it when the behavioral mechanism, not only the wage elasticity, is at stake.
