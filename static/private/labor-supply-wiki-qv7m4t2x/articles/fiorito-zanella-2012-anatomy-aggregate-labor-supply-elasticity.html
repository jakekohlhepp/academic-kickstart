Review of Economic Dynamics - 2012

The Anatomy of the Aggregate Labor Supply Elasticity

A macro-labor paper emphasizing the extensive-margin component of aggregate Frisch elasticities.

Authors
Riccardo Fiorito, Giulio Zanella
Journal
Review of Economic Dynamics
Year

Related themes

indivisible labor and extensive marginmicro vs macro elasticitiesmacro business cycle labor supply

Why it matters

The paper is useful for the wiki because it decomposes aggregate labor-supply elasticity into the margins that actually move. Your note flags its extensive-margin Frisch estimates around 0.8 to 1.4.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Use it as a quantitative counterpart to Rogerson-style indivisible-labor models.
  • Read it alongside Peterman and Erosa-Fuster-Kambourov for extensive-margin contributions.
