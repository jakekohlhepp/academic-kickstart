American Economic Review - 2014

Consumption and Labor Supply with Partial Insurance

A macro-labor paper connecting consumption, labor supply, and incomplete insurance.

Authors
Jonathan Heathcote, Kjetil Storesletten, Giovanni L. Violante
Journal
American Economic Review
Year
2014

Related themes

aggregation heterogeneous elasticitieslife cycle structural estimationmacro business cycle labor supply

Why it matters

The paper places labor supply inside a heterogeneous-agent insurance problem. It is a useful bridge between micro household behavior and aggregate macro models.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Read it with Blundell-Pistaferri-Saporta-Eksten.
  • Use it when labor supply is part of risk sharing rather than only a wage response.
