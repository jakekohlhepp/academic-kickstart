Optimal Redistributive Taxation with Both Extensive and Intensive Responses

An optimal-tax paper emphasizing heterogeneous labor-supply responses across margins.

Authors

Why it matters

The paper belongs in the wiki because optimal-tax formulas are sensitive to which workers respond and on which margin. Heterogeneous intensive-margin elasticities are not a footnote; they change policy tradeoffs.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Pair it with Saez (2001) for the public-finance core.
  • Use it when moving from average elasticities to distribution-sensitive policy design.
