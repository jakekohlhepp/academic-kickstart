Income Taxation in a Life Cycle Model with Human Capital

Why it matters

This entry belongs in the wiki as a bridge between the tax-policy literature and the human-capital labor-supply literature. The central issue is that taxes can change not only hours but also experience accumulation and future wages.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Use it with Imai-Keane and Keane-Wasi when thinking about dynamic tax distortions.
  • Keep it separate from purely static tax-response papers.
