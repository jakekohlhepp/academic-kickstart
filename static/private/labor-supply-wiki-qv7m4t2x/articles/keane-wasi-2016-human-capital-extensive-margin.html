Labour Supply: The Roles of Human Capital and the Extensive Margin

A paper tying labor-supply responses to both human-capital dynamics and participation margins.

Authors
Michael P. Keane, Nada Wasi
Journal
Economic Journal
Year
2016

Related themes

human capital and labor supplyindivisible labor and extensive marginmicro vs macro elasticities

Why it matters

Keane and Wasi connect two issues that often travel separately: work changes future wages, and labor supply may move through participation rather than small hours adjustments.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Read it after Keane (2011) for the narrower human-capital and extensive-margin argument.
  • Use it when a policy question involves entry, exit, and career accumulation at the same time.
