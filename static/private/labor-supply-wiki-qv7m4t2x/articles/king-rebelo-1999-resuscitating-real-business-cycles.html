Why it matters

Real-business-cycle models put the Frisch elasticity at the center of cyclical hours movements. This chapter is a core macro background reference.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Use it as the macro counterpart to the micro Frisch-estimation papers.
  • Read it when asking why macro models sometimes want larger elasticities.
