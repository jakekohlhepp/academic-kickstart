Reconciling Micro and Macro Estimates of the Frisch Labor Supply Elasticity

A reconciliation paper that highlights the contribution of the extensive margin to aggregate Frisch elasticities.

Why it matters

Peterman belongs in the wiki because it quantifies how much of the aggregate Frisch response can come from extensive-margin adjustment. Your note flags an extensive-margin contribution around 0.6 to 0.7.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Read it with Fiorito-Zanella and Erosa-Fuster-Kambourov.
  • Use it when comparing individual hours elasticities with aggregate hours elasticities.
