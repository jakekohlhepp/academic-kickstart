Why it matters

Rogerson and Wallenius make the micro-macro gap a life-cycle and tax problem rather than a simple disagreement about one parameter. Their framework helps explain how small responses at some individual margins can coexist with larger aggregate responses.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Put it between the older Frisch foundations and the Keane-Rogerson reassessment papers.
  • Use it when thinking about taxes that alter lifetime work timing, not only current-period hours.
