Review of Economic Studies - 2001

Using Elasticities to Derive Optimal Income Tax Rates

A foundational optimal-tax paper showing how labor-supply and taxable-income elasticities enter tax formulas.

Why it matters

Saez is a core public-finance reference because it connects empirical elasticities to optimal tax rates. It is one of the clearest reasons the wiki cares about which elasticity is being estimated.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Read it with ETI papers by Feldstein, Gruber-Saez, and Saez-Slemrod-Giertz.
  • Use it when translating labor-supply estimates into welfare and optimal-tax arguments.
