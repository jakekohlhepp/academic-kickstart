Frisch Elasticities in a Model of Indivisible Labor Supply with Endogenous Workweek Length

A recent indivisible-labor paper that lets workweek length be endogenous.

Why it matters

Yum is useful because it does not force the indivisible-labor margin to be a simple on-off choice. Endogenous workweek length helps connect extensive-margin models to observed hours packages.

How to use it in this wiki

  • Pair it with Rogerson (1988) for the foundational model and Chetty et al. (2013) for the empirical extensive-margin benchmark.
  • Use it when the model needs both participation and workweek length.
