Theme page

Compensating differentials and job attributes

Papers where wages, flexibility, hours, amenities, and job attributes are bundled rather than separable.

Why this page matters

If jobs are bundles, a wage elasticity alone can be misleading. Workers may trade pay for flexibility, predictability, remote work, task content, or other amenities.

Reading path

Use Compensating Differentials in Bundles of Job Attributes as the direct entry. Then connect it to the Mas-Pallais alternative-work-arrangements papers, Occupational Preferences Heterogeneity in Labor Supply, and Reservation Wages and Workers' Valuation of Job Flexibility.

Link to firm wage setting

Amenities can flatten or steepen the supply curve facing a firm. That is why this page sits next to monopsony and organization themes.
