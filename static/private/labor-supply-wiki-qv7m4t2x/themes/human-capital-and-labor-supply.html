Theme page

Human capital and labor supply

Papers where work today changes future wages, so labor supply and human-capital accumulation must be modeled together.

The basic idea

If work builds human capital, then current hours affect future wages. That makes the labor-supply problem dynamic even before taxes, family choices, or macro aggregation enter.

Reading path

Start with Life-Cycle Labor Supply with Human Capital Accumulation and Human Capital and Labor Supply: A Synthesis. Then move to Intertemporal Labor Supply and Human Capital Accumulation, Human Capital Accumulation and the Intertemporal Elasticity of Substitution of Labor, and Labour Supply: The Roles of Human Capital and the Extensive Margin.

Why it changes elasticity interpretation

An observed response to wages may mix substitution, career investment, and future wage effects. That is why human-capital papers belong next to Frisch-elasticity and tax-policy papers.
