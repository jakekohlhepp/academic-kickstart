Theme page

Macro and business-cycle labor supply

Macro papers where labor supply helps explain business-cycle hours, cross-country work differences, and aggregate employment.

Why this page exists

Macro labor-supply papers often need aggregate elasticities that are not the same as micro hours elasticities. This page gathers the business-cycle and cross-country references.

Reading path

Use Resuscitating Real Business Cycles for the RBC background. Use Why Do Americans Work So Much More Than Europeans? and Do Taxes Explain European Employment? for cross-country tax arguments. Use Reconciling Cyclical Movements in the Marginal Value of Time and the Marginal Product of Labor and Hours Cyclicality and Labor Supply over the Business Cycle for cyclical hours.

Link to micro-macro

This page is the macro side of the micro-macro elasticity theme. It explains why aggregate models put so much pressure on the elasticity object.
