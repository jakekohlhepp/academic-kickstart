Theme page

Meta-analyses and benchmark estimates

Review and synthesis papers that help calibrate labor-supply elasticities across designs, populations, and margins.

Why this page matters

No single labor-supply estimate should carry the whole field. Meta-analyses and review papers help show how estimates vary by population, design, margin, horizon, and publication selection.

Reading path

Use Intertemporal Substitution in Labor Supply: A Meta-Analysis for Frisch/intertemporal substitution. Use Review of Estimates of the Frisch Elasticity of Labor Supply for applied calibration. Use Does Indivisible Labor Explain the Difference between Micro and Macro Elasticities? for extensive-margin meta-analysis.

Practical use

This page is where to go when a model needs a defensible range rather than a favorite point estimate.
