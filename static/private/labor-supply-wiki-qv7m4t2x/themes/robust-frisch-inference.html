Theme page

Robust inference on Frisch elasticities

A small methods-facing page for uncertainty, robustness, and benchmarking in Frisch elasticity estimation.

What this page is for

This page is for the moment when the question is no longer "what is a Frisch elasticity?" but "how much should I trust the estimate?"

The compact reading path

Robust Inference for the Frisch Labor Supply Elasticity is the direct methods paper. Intertemporal Substitution in Labor Supply: A Meta-Analysis and Review of Estimates of the Frisch Elasticity of Labor Supply provide benchmarking. On the Measurement of the Elasticity of Labour keeps the measurement object visible.

Practical rule

Report the elasticity object, the identifying variation, and the uncertainty together. A precise number without the first two is not very informative.
